Client Portal Terms & Conditions

Please review and accept these terms before accessing your engagement portal.

1. Authorized Access

Access to this client portal is granted exclusively for your organization's engagement with TechGuard Labs. You may access only the project data, findings, and evidence requests that have been explicitly shared with you. Unauthorized access to other clients' data is prohibited.

2. Evidence and Information Uploads

Any documents, files, or information you upload through this portal will be treated as confidential and used solely for the purpose of the named engagement. Do not upload documents containing sensitive personal data beyond what is necessary to fulfil an evidence request. TechGuard Labs will handle all uploaded information in accordance with its data protection obligations.

3. Findings Are Advisory

Findings and risk ratings shared with you through this portal represent professional security assessments at a point in time. They are not legal opinions, compliance certifications, or guarantees of security. Remediation decisions remain the sole responsibility of your organization.

4. Risk Acceptance

If you submit a risk acceptance request for any finding, this is recorded as a formal acknowledgement that your organization is aware of the risk and has chosen not to remediate it at this time. Such acceptance does not limit TechGuard Labs' liability for its assessment. Risk acceptance requests are subject to approval by TechGuard Labs partner-level staff.

5. Confidentiality of Findings

Findings, vulnerabilities, and security gaps disclosed through this portal are confidential. You must not share this information publicly, with insurers, regulators, or third parties without TechGuard Labs' written consent, except as required by law.

6. Activity Logging

All actions you perform in this portal — including logins, file uploads, risk acceptances, and comments — are logged for audit and quality assurance purposes.

7. Data Retention

Your access to this portal and its data will be maintained for the duration of the engagement and a 90-day post-closure period. After this period, access may be revoked and data may be archived or deleted per TechGuard Labs' data retention policy.
